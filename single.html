KCB Launches New Digital Loan Product for SMEs

Fast, flexible financing to support Kenyan small businesses grow

February 26, 2026 KCB Corporate Communications

KCB Group has unveiled a new digital loan product designed specifically for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) across Kenya. The product offers instant approval and disbursement, with flexible repayment terms tailored to business cash flows. This initiative aligns with KCB's commitment to driving economic growth by empowering local businesses with accessible financial solutions.

The new digital loan, named KCB SME QuickLoan, allows businesses to borrow between KES 50,000 and KES 5,000,000 with repayment periods ranging from 3 to 24 months. Eligible businesses include registered SMEs operating for at least 12 months, with access to the loan available via the KCB Mobile App and Internet Banking platforms.

Speaking at the launch event in Nairobi, KCB Group CEO stated: "SMEs are the backbone of Kenya's economy, contributing over 40% to GDP and creating millions of jobs. However, access to affordable financing remains a major challenge for many. With KCB SME QuickLoan, we're leveraging technology to simplify the borrowing process, ensuring businesses can access funds when they need them most."

Additional features of the product include no collateral requirements for loans up to KES 1,000,000, competitive interest rates, and the option to make early repayments without penalties. KCB will also provide free financial management training to all loan recipients through its KCB Business Academy.

To apply for KCB SME QuickLoan, businesses can register on the KCB Mobile App, submit basic business information and bank statements, and receive a decision within minutes. The product is currently available in Kenya, with plans to roll it out to other KCB markets in East Africa later in 2026.
