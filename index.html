KCB Launches New Digital Loan Product for SMEs

Fast, flexible financing to support Kenyan small businesses grow

February 26, 2026 KCB Corporate Communications

KCB Group has unveiled a new digital loan product designed specifically for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) across Kenya. The product offers instant approval and disbursement, with flexible repayment terms tailored to business cash flows. This initiative aligns with KCB's commitment to driving economic growth by empowering local businesses with accessible financial solutions.

KCB Partners with Government to Boost Youth Employment

Financial literacy programs & seed funding for young entrepreneurs

February 20, 2026 KCB Foundation

KCB Foundation has partnered with the Kenyan Ministry of Youth Affairs to launch a nationwide program aimed at reducing youth unemployment. The initiative includes free financial literacy training, mentorship sessions, and seed funding for qualifying youth-led startups. Over 10,000 young Kenyans are expected to benefit in the first year.

Enhanced KCB Mobile App Now Available

New features including biometric login & bill payment integration

February 15, 2026 KCB Digital Banking

The new KCB Mobile App offers customers a seamless banking experience with improved security features, including fingerprint and facial recognition login. Users can now pay utility bills, transfer funds internationally, and manage multiple accounts from a single interface. The app is available for download on both iOS and Android platforms.
